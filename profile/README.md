## What is OSIRIS MD for Mac?

OSIRIS MD for Mac is a professional **DICOM viewer and medical image processing software** designed for radiologists, clinicians, and researchers. It provides advanced tools for viewing, analyzing, and interpreting medical images in DICOM format directly on macOS.

The software supports a wide range of imaging modalities including MRI, CT, X-Ray, and ultrasound, making it a versatile solution for diagnostic and research purposes. With its intuitive interface and powerful processing capabilities, OSIRIS MD enables users to perform measurements, annotations, 3D reconstructions, and more.

Fully optimized for **macOS Ventura, Sonoma, and later versions**, it runs seamlessly on both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs. Whether you are working in a hospital, clinic, or research lab, OSIRIS MD delivers reliable performance and precision.
